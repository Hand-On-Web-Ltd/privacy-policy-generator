# Privacy Policy Generator 🔒

Generate a GDPR-compliant privacy policy for your UK business in about 30 seconds. Fill in a form, get a privacy policy, copy and paste it onto your website.

## Features

- GDPR and UK Data Protection Act 2018 compliant
- Covers: cookies, analytics, contact forms, email marketing, payment processing
- Tick which data types you collect
- Generates clean, readable text
- Copy with one click
- No sign-up, no cost, works in the browser

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html`
2. Fill in your company name, website, and contact email
3. Tick the boxes for the types of data you collect
4. Click "Generate Privacy Policy"
5. Copy the text and add it to your website

## Disclaimer

This generates a good starting point but it's not legal advice. If you handle sensitive data or have complex processing needs, get a solicitor to review it.
